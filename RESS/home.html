RESS (Rulindo Electricity Service Support)

Welcome to the RESS ..

Our mission is to ensure reliable and efficient electricity access for all households in the community. We provide support for new connections, report handling for power outages, and guidance on safe electricity use. Through regular monitoring and service distribution, we help residents manage energy usage effectively and stay informed about billing and maintenance schedules. Our office is committed to improving the quality of life through dependable electricity services.
